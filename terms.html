Do's and Don'ts

Using Sulkan in modpacks

You may include Sulkan in your modpack as long as it is credited and your listing links back to an official page.

Official CurseForge Official Modrinth Official Site
Allowed

Do

Common examples that are explicitly okay.

Add Sulkan to your modpack

Credit Sulkan in your modpack description or documentation.

Link back to official pages

Link to this site, or the official CurseForge / Modrinth pages.

Use the official download sources

Prefer CurseForge or Modrinth so users get updates, dependencies, and changelogs.

Share screenshots and videos

Feel free to showcase Sulkan in your content with attribution.

Not Allowed

Don't

Things that cause confusion or break attribution.

Re-upload Sulkan jars

Do not mirror or re-host Sulkan files on other download sites without permission.

Remove credit or branding

Don’t rename the mod to imply it’s yours, or remove attribution from pack listings.

Claim official affiliation

Don’t present your modpack as “official Sulkan” unless you have explicit approval.

Paywall Sulkan access

Don’t lock downloads behind ads, subscriptions, or gated access.

Need the license text?
