2. Select

Activate a style profile that matches your world tone, cinematic goals, or daily gameplay needs.

3. Fine-tune

4. Publish

Use your final setup for recording, streaming, screenshots, or community server presentations.

Visual pillars

What players notice first

These signature elements shape Sulkan's standout look.

Terrain depth

Landscape shadows create strong depth cues that make biomes and architecture feel more cinematic.

Character presence

Entity shadow treatment adds grounded detail during movement, combat, and close-range scenes.

Water expression

Animated waves and reflections help oceans, rivers, and coastlines become visual highlights.

Skyline drama

Day and night transitions feel more expressive, giving every build a stronger mood profile.
